Investment Philosophy

WAM has exposure to the leading names in the market and remains invested until either the market or portfolio generates a sell signal. We use individual stock rotation, reducing exposure to weaker names while adding exposure to names exhibiting leadership characteristics. We believe in a strategy that combines stock, sector and market as the three categories that in unison can deliver outperformance when aligned.

Our Strategy

Leading Stocks

Our entire system is based off of what we call leaders/leadership. Leaders are the names that have institutional grade buying and are in uptrends. Leaders not only provide directional alpha, but they also provide a feedback loop of overall market health.

Risk Adjusted Returns

Identifying new leaders and thematics is generally the easy part. Finding the risk adjusted entry points that provide good risk to reward, and managing those positions is the much harder part. Our system aims to initiate positions at these point of inflection.

Risk First Approach

Our risk-first approach means that we do not sit through periods of extreme volatility or downtrend with high exposure. Our technology allows us to track internal metrics such as distribution, leadership.

Diversification

There is always a fine line between diversification, and concentration. Too much diversification leads to increased beta, while too little diversification increases single-name risk.
