Our Approach |

Investment Philosophy

Directional alpha can be generated by gaining exposure to quality, leading names/sectors at points of inflection. Market timing techniques ensure both efficient allocation of capital and reduces overall risk, volatility, and smooths equity curve performance over time. Additionally, maintaining higher cash levels during periods of extreme volatility helps safeguard against sustained market downturns and/or blackswan events while allowing flexibility to capitalize on future opportunities.

Our Strategy

Leading Stocks

Our entire system is based off of what we call leaders/leadership. Leaders are the names that have institutional grade buying and are in uptrends. Leaders not only provide directional alpha, but they also provide a feedback loop of overall market health.

Risk Adjusted Returns

Our system focuses on finding risk-adjusted entry points that provide good risk to reward and aim to initiate positions at these point of inflection.

Risk First Approach

Our technology allows us to track internal metrics such as distribution, leadership, and participation, to build an exposure model.

Diversification

Diversification is achieved through geographic allocation, sector coverage, and a balanced mix of ~10-20 alpha-generating positions in each portfolio.
